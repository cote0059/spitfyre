About Us

We genuinely love and enjoy Shiba Inu dogs and we strive to produce quality, healthy Shiba Inu Puppies as family companions as well as show/agility and obedience prospects. Our Shiba Inu Puppies are our best friends; they receive the best care and attention at all times. Our Shiba Inu dogs are kept in our home, in a heated/air conditioned kennel, or with friends or relatives full time.

All of our Shiba Inu puppies and adults are always kept up-to-date on vaccinations. We have had a successful health history with no reports or cases of Hip Dysplasia or Genetic Eye problems. All Pups have a lifetime health and temperament guarantee!
