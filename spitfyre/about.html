A little about us

We are a loving family living in the northern woods of Minnesota. My husband and I have six kids: two are grown and moved out living nearby, two are in the process of moving out, and two are youngsters living with us.
We have been a longtime Minnesota Shiba Inu Breeder with Top Quality AKC Shiba Inu Puppies since 1992. All of my adults are AKC Registered and CH Pedigreed. We have an endless amount of love for our dogs and their pups, spending much of our time working with the dogs and being together as a family. We are family-owned-and-operated and make it our mission to connect great people with a great Shiba Inu puppy.



Our breeding and health

We have had a successful general health history along with no reports or cases of Hip Dysplasia or genetic eye problems. All Pups have a lifetime health and temperament guarantee. Should something happen that you need a refund or replacement, shipping expenses will be covered. We provide a highly competitive health guarantee compared to most kennels; that is how confident I am in my Shiba knowledge, health care, and history!

I do not believe in having a mass amount of dogs to create quantity or volume (known as puppy mills). Puppy mills do not control their breeding or test their breeding stock, which leads to unhealthy pups, faults, and aggressive behavior. They generally do not screen their prospective buyers because they do not care where the puppies go as long as they get their money. I believe in breeding for quality, temperament, and conformation.
Our lifetime health and temperament guarantee entails excellent bloodlines and selective breeding with each and every litter. I only put adults together who will complement each other and create a low chance of faults. I also screen my buyers and make sure that our pups are going to loving homes, and if for any reason a puppy cannot be kept it must be returned to us so we can find it a good home.

We DO NOT sell our Shiba Inu puppies to puppy mills, brokers, or pet stores under any circumstances. I do my best to keep them and their offspring from ending up in puppy mills or in the hands of unethical, uneducated, or backyard breeders. In order to do my part as a responsible breeder, my pet pups are required to be spayed or neutered. We only sell our Shiba Inu Puppies to loving homes as lifetime companions, as well as to responsible breeders whom we can trust to provide safe, healthy, happy homes. Please don't be upset with us if we seem to ask a lot of questions; we are not trying to be nosy, we just want to make sure our Shiba Inu puppies have a loving home for a lifetime.

Please help keep puppies safe by refusing to purchase from puppy mills, pet stores, and especially online brokers who list all breeds of dogs. One indicator is the condition and looks of the dogs; the dogs should be clean, healthy, and within the breed standard.
